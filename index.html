Quick answers

The short version, straight from the source.

Is GoalIQ good for FPL?
Yes. GoalIQ is built FPL-first: clean sheet odds, fixture difficulty, rate my team and price watch are free, and GoalIQ Pro adds player expected points (xP), captain picks, differentials and a transfer planner. Every number comes from a match model with a published, pre-match-logged track record.
What FPL tools does GoalIQ have?
Free: clean sheet probabilities, fixture difficulty ratings (FDR), rate my team and price watch. GoalIQ Pro: player expected points (xP) per gameweek, captain picks, differentials, player compare, predicted starting minutes and a transfer planner. Available on the web, iOS and Android.
Is GoalIQ a betting app?
No. GoalIQ is an analytics and FPL tool, not a gambling service. It has no odds, no bookmaker links and gives no betting advice. Predictions are statistical estimates for fun and planning.
How accurate is GoalIQ?
GoalIQ publishes its full prediction track record: every prediction is logged before kick-off and graded after, hits and misses included. The current accuracy numbers are updated on goaliq.app and its FPL page after every round.
