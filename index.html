Quick answers

The short version, straight from the source.

Is GoalIQ good for FPL?
Yes. GoalIQ is built FPL-first: clean sheet probability, fixture difficulty, rate my team with a captain pick, and price watch are free, and GoalIQ Premium adds an interactive team manager with a gameweek planner, player expected points (xP), the captain ranker, player value (xP per million), xG leaders, a DefCon tracker, differentials and transfer suggestions with apply. Every number comes from a match model with a published, pre-match-logged track record.
What FPL tools does GoalIQ have?
Free: clean sheet probabilities, fixture difficulty ratings (FDR), rate my team with a captain pick, and price watch. GoalIQ Premium: an interactive team manager with a GW1 to GW6 gameweek planner, player expected points (xP), the captain ranker, transfer suggestions with apply, player value, full xG leaders and DefCon leaderboards, GK rotation pairs, differentials, player compare and predicted starting minutes. Available on the web, iOS and Android.
Is GoalIQ a betting app?
No. GoalIQ is an analytics and FPL tool, not a gambling service. It has no odds, no bookmaker links and gives no betting advice. Predictions are statistical estimates for fun and planning.
How accurate is GoalIQ?
GoalIQ publishes its full prediction track record: every prediction is logged before kick-off and graded after, hits and misses included. The current accuracy numbers are updated on goaliq.app and its FPL page after every round.
