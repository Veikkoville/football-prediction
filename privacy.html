Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Veikkoville ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the GoalIQ mobile application ("the App") and related services.

By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Finnish Data Protection Act.

1. Who we are

Data controller: Veikkoville
Country: Finland
Contact: veikkoville@protonmail.com

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password — stored as a salted hash, never in plain text
  • Account creation date

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, date of birth, or any other personal identifiers in the App.

2.2 Subscription and payment information

When you upgrade to GoalIQ Premium, payment is processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not see, store, or have access to your full payment card details. We receive only:

  • Subscription status (active / cancelled)
  • Subscription start and end dates
  • A Stripe customer ID linked to your account

Stripe processes your payment data under their own privacy policy.

2.3 Usage data

We do not currently collect analytics, advertising IDs, device identifiers, or location data. We do not use third-party tracking SDKs.

Recent prediction queries you make in the App are stored locally on your device only (in the App's storage) and never transmitted to our servers for tracking purposes.

3. How we use your information

We use your data only to:

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Provide football match predictions through our API
  • Manage your Premium subscription status
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers, brokers, or other third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

The App relies on the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (hosted in EU) — authentication and user profile storage. Privacy policy
  • Stripe — subscription and payment processing. Privacy policy
  • Render — backend API hosting. Privacy policy
  • Expo / Expo Application Services — mobile app delivery and over-the-air updates. Privacy policy

5. Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records (e.g., billing records for tax purposes — typically 6 years under Finnish law).

6. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi)

To exercise any of these rights, email veikkoville@protonmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Account deletion

You can request account deletion at any time by emailing veikkoville@protonmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

Upon deletion, your account email, password hash, profile data and any locally cached predictions will be permanently removed. Active Stripe subscriptions must be cancelled separately through the App's "Manage subscription" feature or directly in Stripe's Customer Portal.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted database storage, and Row Level Security policies that prevent users from accessing each other's data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International data transfers

Your data is primarily stored within the European Union (Supabase EU region). Some third-party services (such as Stripe and Render) may process data outside the EU. In such cases, those providers comply with GDPR-approved data transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses).

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email when possible.

12. Disclaimer about predictions

GoalIQ provides statistical predictions for football matches based on machine learning models. These predictions are provided for informational and entertainment purposes only and do not constitute betting advice or guarantee any outcomes. The App does not facilitate gambling or accept wagers.
