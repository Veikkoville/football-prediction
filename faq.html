What is GoalIQ?

GoalIQ is a statistical football match prediction tool. You select two teams and the app uses machine learning models (Dixon-Coles + LightGBM ensemble) to estimate match outcomes, expected goals (xG), and likely scorelines.

GoalIQ is not a betting platform. It is an analytics tool for football fans who want to understand match probabilities. Predictions are statistical estimates, never guarantees.

  2. Tap Manage subscription.
  3. The Stripe Customer Portal opens — tap Cancel plan and confirm.

Your Premium access remains active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again, and you can re-subscribe at any time.

How accurate are the predictions?

Predictions are probabilistic, not deterministic. A 60 % home-win probability means that, across many similar matches, the home team would win roughly 60 % of the time — it does not mean this specific match will be a home win.

The underlying models are trained on historical match data (results, xG, recent form) and validated against past seasons. We tune the model continuously as more data arrives.

Can I use GoalIQ for betting?

GoalIQ is an analytics tool, not a betting platform. We do not place bets, accept wagers, or partner with bookmakers. What you choose to do with the statistical estimates is up to you — but we recommend treating them as one data point among many, not as guaranteed outcomes.
