What is GoalIQ?

GoalIQ is a statistical football match prediction tool. You select two teams and the app uses machine learning models (Dixon-Coles + LightGBM ensemble) to estimate match outcomes, expected goals (xG), and likely scorelines.

GoalIQ is not a betting platform. It is an analytics tool for football fans who want to understand match probabilities. Predictions are statistical estimates, never guarantees.

Which leagues are supported?
  • Premier League: all current-season matchups, available now.
  • FIFA World Cup 2026: launches 11 June 2026 when the tournament begins.

We plan to add La Liga, Bundesliga and Serie A after launch.

GoalIQ Premium is billed monthly through your app store (Apple App Store or Google Play) at the price shown in the app for your region. You can cancel anytime.

Premium unlocks:

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

GoalIQ Premium is billed through your app store, so you cancel in your device's subscription settings:

  • iOS: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → GoalIQ → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → GoalIQ → Cancel.

You can also tap Manage subscription in the app's Profile tab, which opens the same settings. Your Premium access remains active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again, and you can re-subscribe at any time.

GoalIQ Pro (web): if you subscribed at pro.goaliq.app, email veikkoville@protonmail.com and we cancel it immediately. Access stays active until the end of the paid period.

What is GoalIQ Pro on the web?

GoalIQ Pro is a web dashboard at pro.goaliq.app built for Fantasy Premier League. The free view shows clean sheet odds and model-based fixture difficulty for every PL team. Pro adds player expected points (xP) for the coming gameweeks, a captain ranker and per-gameweek breakdowns, all from the same GoalIQ match model with a published track record.

Pricing: 3.99 €/month or 25 €/year (season pass). Sign in with your GoalIQ account, the same one you use in the app.

Does one subscription work on both the web and the app?

Yes. One account, premium on web, iOS and Android. If you subscribe to GoalIQ Pro on the web, premium unlocks in the mobile app when you sign in with the same account. If you already have Premium through the app store, sign in at pro.goaliq.app and Pro is active there too, no second purchase.

How accurate are the predictions?

Predictions are probabilistic, not deterministic. A 60 % home-win probability means that, across many similar matches, the home team would win roughly 60 % of the time. It does not mean this specific match will be a home win.

The underlying models are trained on historical match data (results, xG, recent form) and validated against past seasons. We tune the model continuously as more data arrives.
