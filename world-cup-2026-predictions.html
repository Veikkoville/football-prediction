How the model works

GoalIQ is powered by a Dixon-Coles statistical model combined with a machine-learning expected-goals (xG) ensemble, trained on thousands of real international and club matches. For any fixture it estimates the win / draw / win probability, the expected goals for each team, and the most likely exact scorelines. Predictions for World Cup 2026 are computed on a neutral-venue basis and refresh as new results come in during the tournament.
