How accurate are GoalIQ football predictions?
GoalIQ publishes its full prediction track record: every prediction is logged before kick-off and graded against the final result afterwards, hits and misses included, with no edits. The current accuracy numbers are shown on this page and refreshed after every round, and the raw log is available as machine-readable JSON.
How does GoalIQ predict football matches?
GoalIQ uses a Dixon-Coles statistical model combined with a machine-learning expected-goals (xG) ensemble, trained on thousands of real club and international matches. For any fixture it estimates the win probability for each side and the draw, expected goals for both teams, and the probability of the most likely scorelines.
Is GoalIQ a betting app?
No. GoalIQ is an analytics tool, not a gambling service. It has no bookmaker links and gives no betting advice. Predictions are statistical estimates for fun, analysis and fantasy football planning.
